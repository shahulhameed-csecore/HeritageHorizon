Day 01 : Arrive Chennai – Tiruthani – Kumbakonam


Arrive at Chennai airport and drive to Tiruttani to visit Thiruthani Murugan Temple. Then, drive to Kumbakonam and stay overnight at Kumbakonam.

Day 02 : Kumbakonam – Palani – Madurai


After breakfast, visit Swamimalai Murugan Temple, then drive to Palani via Trichy. Upon arrival in Palani, Visit Palanimalai Murugan Temple. Then, drive to Madurai for an overnight stay.

Day 03 : Madurai – Tiruchendur – Madurai


After breakfast, take a day excursion to Tiruchendur Murugan Temple and return to Madurai for an overnight stay.

Day 04 : Madurai – Chennai


Morning visit to Pazhamudhircholai Murugan Temple and Tiruparankundram Murugan Temple, then you will be transferred to Madurai airport or railway station for your onward journey back home.
