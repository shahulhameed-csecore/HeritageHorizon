Day 01 : Chennai


On your arrival at Chennai Airport, you will be met and greeted by our tour executive. On arrival, check in at the hotel and relax. Later, visit Parthasarathy Temple, Kapaleeshwarar Temple, and Ashtalakshmi Temple; overnight stay in Chennai.

Day 02 : Chennai to Tirupathi via Thiruthani


Day 02 : Chennai to Tirupathi via Thiruthani After breakfast, you will proceed towards Tirupati and, en route, visit the Tiruttani Murugan temple (one of the six abodes of Lord Murugan). On arrival, check in at the hotel and relax. Later, visit Lord Balaji Temple, which is the richest temple in the world, during your trip to Tamil Nadu, and overnight stay at Tiruttani.

Day 03 : Tirupathi to Tiruvannamalai via Vellore


This day you will proceed towards Tiruvannamalai, en route visit the Vellore Golden Temple of Holistic Tamilnadu, and overnight stay at Tiruvannamalai.

Day 04 : Tiruvannamalai to Chidambarams


Morning visit Lord Arunachaleshwarar temple (one of the five elements temples; this temple represents fire) and proceed towards Chidambaram. On arrival, check in at the hotel and relax. Later, visit the Lord Nataraja temple (one of the five elements of temples; this temple represents the sky), and overnight stay at Chidambaram.

Day 05 : Chidambaram to Kumbakonam


After breakfast, proceed towards Kumbakonam and, en route, visit some of the Navagraha temples like Vaitheeswarankoil, Thiruvenkadu, Keezha Perumpallam, and Thirunallar. After dharsan, you will check in at the hotel in Kumbakonam and overnight stay at Kumbakonam.

Day 06 : Kumbakonam to Rameshwaram via Tanjore


Early in the in the morning, visit Swamimalai Murugan temple (one of the six abodes of Lord Murugan) and proceed towards Rameshwaram, one of the holy places of Hindus. En route, visit Brihadishvara Temple (the big temple) at Tanjore during the period of the trip to Tamil Nadu and overnight stay at Rameswaram.

Day 07 : Rameshwaram to Madurai


In the morning, you shall visit Lord Ramanathaswamy temple. After Dharsan, proceed towards Madurai. On arrival, check in at the hotel and relax. Later, you shall visit Meenakshi Temple and Mariamman Temple and overnight stay at Madurai.

Day 08 : Madurai


This day you shall do sightseeing in Madurai, which includes Alagar Temple (this temple is dedicated to Lord Vishnu), Pazhamudircholai Murugan Temple (one of the six abodes of Lord Murugan), and Thiruparankundram Murugan Temple (one of the six abodes of Lord Murugan).

Day 09 : Madurai to Trichy via Palani


Early in the in the morning, proceed towards Trichy. En route, you shall visit Palani Murugan Temple (one of the six abodes of Lord Murugan). On arrival, check in at the hotel and relax. Later, you shall visit Srirangam temple and Jambukeswarar temple (one of the five elements temples; this temple represents water). You will overnight stay at Trichy.

Day 10 : Trichy to Chennai


This day you will proceed towards Chennai. On arrival, check in at the hotel and relax. If time permits, you shall go shopping in the T. Nagar area. Overnight stay in Chennai.

Day 11 : Chennai


This day, you will be transferred to Chennai Airport for your onward journey home with sweet memories.
