Day 02 : Coimbatore - Indira Gandhi Wildlife Sanctuary and National Park


We will head to Mudumalai. This place is about 160 Km from Coimbatore. Take some rest in the hotel and then we will start for Mudumalai Wildlife Sanctuary. This sanctuary is a real beauty offered to you by nature. Rich in flora and fauna, this place is a heaven for the adventure lovers. You can easily spot the animals like leopard, Chital, Sloth bear, barking deer, wild dogs, and avian population in this park is also significant. Small hillock and many water bodies add to the glory of this place. Bamboo, Terminalia, Grewia, Ardesia etc are the plants species that can be found in abundance in this park. Dinner and stay will be in the resorts of Mudumalai.
